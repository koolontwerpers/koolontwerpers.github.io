KOOL

We are a collective of independent creative entrepreneurs. Together we share an office space, collaborate on occasion and like to be surrounded by lots of plants. Founded in 2009 we have continuously been the home to many Rotterdam based businesses from startups to well established. Currently we have touched down in the zoho area.

Our members have a widespread network in professional creativity. Combined they can provide the needed expertise for internal and external collaborations in graphic, fashion, surface pattern, interior & product design, architecture, urban planning, photography & creative technology.
